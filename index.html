Dosing recommendations
Transcendence Pools reminders:
  • Never mix chemicals together — add one at a time.
  • Always mix chemicals with water first, then pour into the weir.
  • For alkalinity increaser, add maximum 500g at a time.
  • Add pool acid in the morning, chlorine at night.
  • Shock your pool once a month during swimming season.
  • Run pump at least 12 hrs/day in summer, 8 hrs/day in winter.
  • Retest water 24 hours after any chemical treatment.
